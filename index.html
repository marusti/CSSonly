CSSonly is a lightweight pure CSS UI kit, which works completely without jQuery and without Javascript. With CSSOnly, websites can be created quickly and easily. Of course, all components are responsive and follow the mobile-first" approach. Due to the flexibility, layouts can easily be individually designed.

Due to its simple structure, CSSonly is suitable for both beginners and advanced users.

Some of the CSSonly components are featured below. For a full list and complete demo, please visit the CSSonly Demo page.
